The Diary

Academic Project • 2024

Academic UX/UI Design Figma Prototype Storytelling

Overview

"The Diary" is a speculative design project that explores the illusion of digital erasure through the fictional narrative of Sofia, a university student and cybercrime survivor. The physical diary uses visible and UV-reactive ink to represent public and hidden digital traces.

My Role

I served as the project manager, led background research, developed the fictional persona, and wrote the diary entries. I also contributed significantly to worldbuilding and narrative structure.

Key Features

  • Interactive diary with visible and UV-reactive layers
  • Worldbuilding around digital surveillance and privacy
  • Speculative storytelling and physical prototyping
